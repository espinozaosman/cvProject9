Beyond All Expectations

Warehousedirectusa.com was established in 2000 and is currently privately owned and founded in Atlanta, GA. warehousedirectusa.com is staffed by friendly, knowledgeable and furniture savvy individuals. We have over 15 years experience selling furniture on the web. We are equipped to handle any situation that may arise.

We understand that purchasing furniture online can seem a bit risky. It is our mission to create a safe and secure marketplace for each of our customers. Our customers should feel free to contact us anytime, 7 days a week, and as often as you'd like to ensure that you feel 100% comfortable with your purchase. We at warehousedirectusa.com appreciate your trust, and look forward to making your experience with us not only worthwhile, but safe and pleasant as well. Nearly 13% of our business comes from repeat customers. We have no doubt that our percentage of repeat business will continue to grow.

Our mission at warehousedirectusa.com is simple: provide personal service, convenience, competitive pricing and a broad selection of merchandise to furnish any desired space. By succeeding with that mission, it is our goal for every customer to enjoy their buying experience with us and refer us to their friends and family.

Enjoy shopping from our store!
